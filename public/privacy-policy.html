Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

NewU ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Email address and name (for account creation)
  • Addiction type and quit date (for tracking your recovery)
  • Progress photos (stored locally on your device)
  • Usage data (to improve the app)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalize the NewU recovery experience
  • To calculate your sobriety progress and savings
  • To send daily check-in notifications (only if enabled)
  • To process subscription payments via Stripe

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL database). Progress photos are stored locally on your device and never uploaded to our servers. Payment processing is handled by Stripe — we never store your card details.

4. AI Companion (Nova)

Conversations with Nova are processed by Anthropic's Claude API. We do not store your conversation history on our servers. Conversations are processed in real-time and not used for training AI models.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Anthropic — AI companion (Nova)

6. Data Retention

Your account data is retained as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Children's Privacy

NewU is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at mariosraptor@gmail.com.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification.
